Corn Muffins

Description

A simple but delicious corn muffin recipe that can easily be dressed up by adding cheese, jalape√±os, honey, or anything else.

Ingredients

  • Cornmeal
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • Egg
  • Oil
  • Milk
  1. Mix cornmeal, flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt together in a large bowl, mix together
  2. Add egg, oil, and milk; stir gently to combine.
  3. Spoon batter into prepared muffin cups.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into a muffin comes out clean, about 15 to 20 minutes.
